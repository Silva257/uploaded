RULES AND REGULATIONS GOVERNING THE ASSOCIATION

  1. Monthly meetings (savings) shall be taking place on Every last Saturday of the month; From 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  2. SAVINGS will run for 12 Months
  3. Abstention to the sitting shall be fined 5000 ugx regardless of the reason.
  4. Any delay to the meeting shall be fined : 2000 ugx.
  5. The amount of saving will be as follows:
    20000 of saving + 5000 of welfare; Or
    30000 of saving + 5000 of welfare.
  6. Every member is supposed to take a loan,.. Failure to do so will lead him/her to share nothing from the interest made through the loans.
  7. The welfare money shall be borrowed at zero interest, Shall be taken back as borrowed.
  8. The interest from the loan taken shall be of 10%.
  9. Registration fee for new member in the association is : 100000 ugx (one hundred thousand ugx).
    That money shall be paid in cash before admission (Entry). That money is not returnable. Being a Medical worker is a must.
  10. Savings shall be done on the days of sitting only; For proper accountability and recordings.
  11. The amount of welfare money borrowed shall not exceed 100000 ugx.
    The welfare taken shall be returned within 2 months.
  12. Contributions towards the loss of someone's biological person shall be of 20000 ugx only.
  13. For Critical medical condition (disease) of a member : The contribution will be of : 10000 ugx. For biological family relative of a member in the above situation ; contribution is : 5000 ugx.
  14. Loans taken shall all be paid before December.
  15. No loans shall be given out from the month of October.
